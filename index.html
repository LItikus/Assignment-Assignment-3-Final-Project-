How are you doing?

My name is Jama. I`m from Tajikistan. I`m a professional concept artist,illustrator and a mattepainter currently working for movie and game industry in London, UK. I studied Aerospace Engineering, but in my mid-twenties i decided to switch over to concept art and i`m loving it so far. I`m at my mid-thirties now and moving towards art-directing and directing. Recently i`ve worked on such movies as Avengers: Age of Ultron, X-men: Days of future past, Guardians of the Galaxy and many others.
